What's The Story?¶

COVID-19 in the USA: Things you didn't know.¶

We were motivated to analyse COVID-19 data to understand¶

  1. What has happened so far.
  2. The impact on different parts of the country.
  3. The factors driving regional differences.

Why? Because it is highly topical, personally relevant, and a useful test of our skills.¶

What Data?¶

Data Sources¶

  1. Cases and Deaths by County from USAFacts.org, aggregating data from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), state- and local-level public health agencies.
  2. Census Data from Census.gov.
  3. Healthcare data from HRSA.gov.

Data Cleanup¶

  1. Cases and Deaths data required clean up prior to merging, esp. as county names were different.
  2. HSRA data needed extensive wrangling to isolate a county-level score.
  3. Census API took some work to understand.

Sneak Peak¶

  1. Charts of which areas have been most and least affected
  2. Maps of where the virus has spread. And where it hasnt reached.
  3. Statistical Analysis of demographic factors driving case rates.

  3. There are also clearly other factors at work.
